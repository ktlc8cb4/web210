Our Founder

Phuoc Hai Temple was founded by Master Minh Nghiem in 2006. She has started to practice Buddhist when she was 25 years old. She had founded Giac Tam Temple in Minnesota in 2000. In 2006, she moved to North Carolina and founded Phuoc Hai Temple in Charlotte.

She has eight siblings, four of whom are Buddhist monks who have founded their own temples in California and Georgia. One of her sisters currently practices Buddhism with her at Phuoc Hai Temple.

Our Community

We currently have a small community of about 160 people who regularly come to participate in our activities. We strive to bring well known masters from around the world to give Dharma Talks at our place. Despite us being a small community, we have had many successful events and have received strong support from our fellow Buddhists from places such as Canada, Vietnam, and other states. We are currently building a larger temple to be able to accommodate more people. We have broken ground in 2017 and plan on moving in 2020.

Our Mission

Our purpose is to serve as spiritual guidance to our community member who seek to learn about Buddhism.
