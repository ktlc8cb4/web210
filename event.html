Announcement

To better protect you in the mid of the Coronavirus Pandemic, we would like to inform you that Phuoc Hai Temple will temporarily stop the following activities: Dharma Talks, Canon Classes, Worship Sessions, and Buddhist Holidays. We will keep this page updated of the changes in our schedules and events.

In this troubled time, we hope you and your family find the spiritual strength in the Dharma to give each other comfort and to stay safe.

Even though we are not gathering to study the Dharma, we are here to help you answer questions that you might have.

You can call us anytime at 704-394-6896.

You can also email us at phuochaitemple@yahoo.com.

We also have a FQA Page as a resource for you to post any questions or comments. We will do our best to have an answer for your question.
